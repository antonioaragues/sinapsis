description: Generate a weekly review of wiki activity, decisions, and follow-ups
---

Generate a weekly review covering the last 7 days:

1. Read `wiki/log.md` for activity in the last 7 days
2. Identify:
   - New sources ingested
   - New entities, decisions, comparisons created
   - Entities/decisions whose timelines were updated
   - Open action items (see `wiki/action-items.md`)
   - Notable contradictions or open questions
3. Synthesize a concise review with sections: Activity, Decisions Moved, Knowledge Added, Open Threads
4. Save to `outputs/briefings/weekly-review-YYYY-MM-DD.md`
5. Append to `wiki/log.md`
